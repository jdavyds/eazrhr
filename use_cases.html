Businesses

A software development company in the heart of Lagos has had to reduce its employees’ physical attendance at work in line with the Covid-19 directive. However, it has been difficult to monitor their health and general well-being, as well as their availability for work. This has slashed the staff productivity by over 50% and made it difficult to calculate their work attendance and remuneration accurately.

Solution

They used Eazr Employee Management feature with a workflow that allows employees to—

  • Apply for leave/remote work.
  • Specify the reason for the request from a dropdown list.
  • Request start and end dates
  • Answer a series of pre-programmed questions that determine the consideration their request will be given.
  • Receive weekly emails that check on their condition while they are away from the office.
  • Report the progress of their remote tasks/projects as well as hindrances like internet signal, phone network issues, etc.

Benefits

  • Real-time workflow monitoring through centralised tracking and reporting
  • Secure storage in the Cloud and ease of accessing automatically archived data for auditing

Outsourcing Firms

An outsourcing firm is challenged with the need to accelerate its employee onboarding process to accommodate the growing demand for marketers in the banking industry.

Solution

  • Eazr’s self-service feature which allows HR managers from the outsourcing firm to submit onboarding requests.
  • Documents and processes are tailored to the company’s requirements using intuitive form and workflow design interfaces.
  • Onboarding requests are automatically routed for proper review and approval.
  • Orientation and training programs are scheduled through the workflow and supported by features such as reminders/notifications.
  • Newly employed staff can easily receive files containing orientation materials and onboarding documents, append signatures on them electronically, and submit them back.

Benefits

  • Expedited response times and new employee recruiting/onboarding
  • Less manual paperwork, leading to less errors and greater efficiency
  • Integration of e-signature standardises secure approvals.
