About EAZR

We are redefining HR through the design of alternative solutions for employee management.

Eazr is an innovative platform designed to streamline human resource operations and improve employer-employee relationships.

We offer automated solutions to services required by small and large organisations through our freemium products which are rendered in web and mobile application formats.

These products are built with features that provide Eazr solutions to your HR processes, such as employee management, payroll, and documentation, giving you time to focus on actual productive work.

Through our highly intuitive self-service features, employees can also participate in their own onboarding and documentation. They can interact with the company and with their colleagues, thus establishing a more cohesive work culture.

Why choose Eazr?

Accuracy and Efficiency

Designed with the peculiar needs of your business in mind, our APIs integrate seamlessly into your other work tools.

Also, Eazr’s automated HR features ensure you record zero errors in your deliverables without any extra effort from your team.

Strong Industry Presence

Enjoy the advantages of an IT compliant-company with Eazr.

Our paperless approach to employee record management and documentation makes for a tidy, progressive, and more productive HR operation, reinforcing your company’s reputation for solid expertise.

Staff Development

Our highly intuitive self-service feature allows for efficient communication and data sharing between your business and employees. This eliminates the dichotomy between your staff and the HR team, improving cohesion and involvement.
