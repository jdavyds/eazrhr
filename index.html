About EAZR

Eazr is an innovative platform designed to streamline human resource operations and improve employer-employee relationships.

We offer automated HR solutions to small and large organisations through our freemium products which are rendered in web and mobile application formats.

Our product is built with features that provide Eazr (easier) solutions to your HR processes, such as employee management, payroll, and documentation, giving you time to focus on actual productive work.

Through our highly intuitive self-service features, employees can also participate in their own onboarding and documentation. They can interact with the company and with their colleagues, thus establishing a more cohesive work culture.
